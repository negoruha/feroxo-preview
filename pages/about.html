PROVEN IN
PRACTICE

Since 2018, we have supplied and supported SHINDAIWA diesel
welding generators, delivering more than 170 units now operating
across oil & gas, municipal, and rail infrastructure projects

This experience is the foundation of Feroxo in the Czech Republic. We
know how SHINDAIWA performs where it matters most - on real job
sites, in rain, freezing temperatures, and round-the-clock operations

JAPANESE
