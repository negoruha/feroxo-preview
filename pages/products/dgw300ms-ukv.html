DGW300MS/UKV

Diesel Welder │ Part No. 123456

The DGW300MS/UKV is a diesel-powered welder generator designed for construction, maintenance, fabrication, and field service applications. Combining reliable welding performance with auxiliary power generation, the unit enables efficient operation in remote locations and on demanding job sites.

Powered by a durable diesel engine, the machine delivers stable welding output for a wide range of welding tasks while providing AC power for tools and site equipment. Its robust construction is designed to withstand harsh working environments and continuous industrial use.
